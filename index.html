About Us

Love Arena Ministries, led by Pastor Ephantus Njogu, is a Christ-centered family in Nairobi, Kenya. We are passionately committed to bringing hope to the hopeless, healing to the broken, and transformation to lives through the unchanging Gospel of Jesus Christ. At Love Arena Ministries, we believe in the power of God’s love to restore, uplift, and guide every person toward a life of purpose and wholeness.
